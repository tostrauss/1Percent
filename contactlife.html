Meet Our Coaches

Nathan Tau

Soccer Technical Coach & Personal Trainer

Background: Nathan grew up in Botswana then England where he developed his passion for soccer from an early age. His technical skills and dedication earned him a soccer scholarship to Post University in the United States, where he further refined his game and understanding of soccer techniques.

Coaching Philosophy: Nathan believes in developing players with strong technical foundations. His training sessions focus on mastering ball control, enhancing vision, and improving decision-making on the field.

Specialties: Technical skills development, attacking play, midfield organization, and sports-specific fitness training.

Luca Winterton

Tactical Coach & Fitness Specialist

Background: Luca was raised in England where he built a strong foundation in soccer. His tactical understanding and leadership qualities led him to Western Connecticut State University (WestConn) on a soccer scholarship, where he captained the team and developed an analytical approach to the game.

Coaching Philosophy: Luca focuses on the tactical and mental aspects of soccer. He helps players understand game scenarios, positional awareness, and team dynamics while developing the physical attributes needed for peak performance.

Specialties: Tactical development, defensive organization, strength and conditioning, and sports psychology.
