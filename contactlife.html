Meet Our Coaches

Learn more about the experienced coaches dedicated to helping you improve.

Nathan Tau

Soccer Technical Coach & Personal Trainer

Background: Nathan grew up playing in Botswana and England before earning a soccer scholarship to Post University (NCAA DII) in Waterbury, CT. His focus is on the technical foundations vital for success.

Coaching Philosophy: Develop technically proficient players through mastering ball control, enhancing field vision, and improving decision-making under pressure.

Specialties: Dribbling & ball mastery, first touch refinement, attacking movements, midfield control, sport-specific fitness.

Certifications:

  • USSF 'C' License
  • Certified Personal Trainer - NASM
  • Speed & Agility Specialist

Luca Winterton

Tactical Coach & Fitness Specialist

Background: Raised in England, Luca developed a strong tactical understanding of the game. He captained the Western Connecticut State University (WestConn - NCAA DIII) soccer team, bringing leadership and analytical skills.

Coaching Philosophy: Focus on tactical intelligence, positional awareness, and mental toughness, combined with building the physical attributes for peak performance.

Specialties: Tactical analysis, defensive organization, team shape, strength & conditioning, speed & agility training.

Certifications:

  • NSCAA Advanced National Diploma
  • Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist - CSCS
  • Youth Mental Performance Coach

Pablo Tenorio

Technical Specialist & Organizer

Background: Hailing from Barcelona, Spain, Pablo brings the renowned Spanish technical style and tactical organization to our training program. His experience in La Liga youth academies provides unique insights into the beautiful game.

Coaching Philosophy: Emphasizes the importance of technical precision, spatial awareness, and intelligent movement. Believes in creating organized, fluid playing systems that maximize player creativity.

Specialties: Tiki-taka methodology, possession-based play, positional rotations, team coordination, small-sided games, technical skills refinement.

Certifications:

  • UEFA 'B' License
  • Spanish Football Federation Youth Coaching Certificate
  • Sports Psychology & Team Dynamics Specialist
